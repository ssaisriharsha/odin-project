Veg burger

Description

A veggie burger is a popular plant-based alternative to traditional meat burgers, offering a delicious and nutritious option for vegetarians and vegans alike. Typically, it features a patty made from ingredients like black beans, chickpeas, lentils, quinoa, or a blend of vegetables and grains, seasoned with herbs and spices for flavor. The patty is often grilled, baked, or pan-fried until crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. It's served on a bun alongside classic burger toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayo. Some variations may include cheese, avocado, or caramelized onions for added taste and texture. Veggie burgers are not only satisfying but also versatile, allowing for endless customization to suit personal preferences and dietary needs while providing a wholesome alternative to meat-based burgers.

Ingredients

  • Patty
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Condiments
  • Cheese slices
  • Avocado
