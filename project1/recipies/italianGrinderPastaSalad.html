Italian grinder pasta salad

Description

Italian grinder pasta salad is a hearty and flavorful dish that combines the elements of a classic Italian grinder sandwich with the freshness of a pasta salad. It features pasta, usually rotini or penne, tossed with a variety of ingredients typically found in an Italian grinder, such as sliced salami, pepperoni, and ham. Fresh vegetables like cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, red onions, and black olives add color and crunch. The salad is often dressed with a zesty Italian vinaigrette, made from olive oil, vinegar, garlic, and Italian herbs, which ties all the flavors together. Provolone or mozzarella cheese cubes are also common additions, providing a creamy texture. This pasta salad is perfect for picnics, potlucks, or as a refreshing, make-ahead meal that captures the savory, tangy, and herbaceous flavors of an Italian deli sandwich in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salami
  • Pepperoni
  • Ham
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Bell pepper
  • Red onion
  • Black olives
  • Mozzarella cheese
