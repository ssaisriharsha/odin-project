Fried rice

Description

Veg fried rice is a delicious and colorful dish that combines fluffy rice stir-fried with an assortment of fresh vegetables. Commonly used vegetables include carrots, peas, bell peppers, and onions, providing both texture and vibrant colors to the dish. The rice is typically cooked beforehand and then stir-fried in a wok or skillet with the vegetables, seasoned with soy sauce, garlic, and sometimes ginger for added flavor. This dish is known for its simplicity and versatility, making it a favorite in Asian cuisines and beyond. It can be enjoyed as a standalone meal or paired with other dishes like tofu or spring rolls. Veg fried rice is appreciated for its satisfying taste, ease of preparation, and ability to accommodate personal preferences by adjusting the mix of vegetables and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ginger
  • Spring onions
